About us

We are the flagship brand of the Inditex group. Few clothing brands keep up with the latest fashion, are of high quality and yet, affordable. we are probably the amalgamation of all these qualities that made Zara, the Spanish clothing brand become the go-to fashion brand for all.

MATERIALS & CARE

We work with monitoring programmes to ensure compliance with safety, health and quality standards for our products. Caring for your clothes is caring for the environment. Lower temperature washes and delicate spin cycles are gentler on garments and help to protect the colour, shape and structure of the fabric. Furthermore, they reduce the amount of energy used in care processes.
